Graduate Underemployment in Sri Lanka

Academic Research Survey  |  Estimated time: 8–10 minutes  |  Anonymous & Confidential

Section 0
Introduction & Consent
This questionnaire is part of an academic research study on graduate underemployment in Sri Lanka. The purpose is to understand how underemployment relates to work experiences, engagement, and intentions to seek opportunities abroad. Your responses are anonymous and confidential. The survey will take approximately 8–10 minutes to complete.
